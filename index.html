Kawasaki Ninja 250R

2008


The Ninja 250R is a practical, reliable, easy to operate, badass sportbike. Ideal first bike with all the big bike features.
#pocketrocket

SPECS

Displacement: 249cc
Engine: 4-stroke parallel twin, liquid-cooled
Max power: 24 kW {33 PS} / 11,000 rpm
Transmission: 6-speed
Front suspension: 37mm telescopic fork
Rear suspension: Bottom-Link Uni-Trak with gas-charged shock, adjustable 5-way preload
Front brake: 290mm disc, two-piston caliper
Rear brake: 220mm disc, two-piston caliper
Dry weight: 152 kg
Fuel capacity: 17 litres
Seat height: 790 mm
Top speed: 170km/h

PICTURES

In the city or on twisty country roads, the lightweight, rider-friendly Ninja 250R is a blast to ride!
This model was developed to offer street sport performance to riders of all skill levels.
